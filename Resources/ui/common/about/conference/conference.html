About R3 2012

The 2012 AITP Region 3 Student Conference is a highly-anticipated, annual event that brings together AITP members, both professionals and students alike, from all over the South region. The conference will be held October 11 - 13, 2012, in Lafayette, Louisiana and hosted by the University of Louisiana at Lafayette's own AITP student chapter.

The conference features dozens of curated sessions and panels from some of the most influential people and brightest minds within the AITP community and beyond, as well as countless opportunities for networking, competitions, informal conversations, and more.
