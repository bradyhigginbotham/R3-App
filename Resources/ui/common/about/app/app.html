About this Application

The 2012 R3 mobile application lets you view conference schedules, competitions, certifications, maps, and presenter bios from the palm of your hand, as well as live updates from the official R3 Conference Twitter feed. The 2012 R3 mobile application for iPhone and Android devices was developed by Louisiana at Lafayette's own AITP students.
